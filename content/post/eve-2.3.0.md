[Eve v2.3](https://pypi.org/project/Eve/2.3.0/) was just released on PyPI. It adds `optimize_pagination_for_speed`, a resource-level setting that allows granular control, overriding the equivalent global option that goes by the same name. Many thanks to Emanuele Di Giacomo for [contributing](https://github.com/pyeve/eve/pull/1569) to the project.
